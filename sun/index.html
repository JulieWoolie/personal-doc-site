Delphi Arcadius

Realm of The Divine Sun

Above us shines the great sun of a hundred names
It grants us eternal warmth and affection.
Our crops it feeds and our lives it enables.
Our greatest lord, the one who always rises,
Who gives us consistency when there is nought else
Shower us with thine warmth and light
So that we may prosper.
